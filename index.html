The Stars

The stars are higher than the sky
The earth is lower than the sky
So the earth compared to the stars
Is unreachable
You can't reach the stars from where we are
It's impossible
They always say it doesn't hurt to try
But if it's impossible
How can we do the impossible
Just to reach the stars
